Who We are

The Phombo–Awajung family represents a shared lineage rooted in Limbu tradition, ancestry, and collective responsibility.

Across generations, our people have lived and contributed in different regions, yet our identity remains united through Bangshawali, culture, and respect for our forefathers.

Phombo Awajung Kalyankari Samaj

Phombo Awajung Kalyankari Samaj was established in 2019 with the vision of uniting all Phombo Awajung families across Nepal and around the world.

The organization exists to help members recognize one another as one extended family and to build lasting connections across generations.

Through collective effort, the Samaj works to preserve Limbu culture,traditions, and values, ensuring they are passed on to younger generations while strengthening unity among our people.

Objectives of Phombo Awajung Kalyankari Samaj:

  • Unity and Identification To unite all Phombo Awajung families across Nepal and the world, helping members identify one another as one extended family and strengthening bonds across generations.
  • Preservation of Culture and Lineage To preserve and promote Limbu culture, traditions, language, and Bangshawali (genealogical lineage), ensuring that younger generations understand their roots and heritage.
  • Mutual Support and Welfare To provide financial, moral, and social support to members during unforeseen circumstances such as illness, death, or hardship, through collective contribution and community solidarity.
